## Appendix: x402 Market Context

### What is x402?
HTTP 402 "Payment Required" status code, dormant since 1997, revived by Coinbase + Cloudflare in Sept 2025 as a protocol for instant, autonomous stablecoin payments (USDC, USDT) directly over HTTP.

### Market Size
- $600M volume in first 2 months (Nov 2025)
- Pantera Capital predicting business intelligence agents on x402 in 2026
- Major adopters: Google Cloud, AWS, Anthropic, Cloudflare

### BlockRun's Position
Trust layer sitting between payment requests and execution - verifies identity, checks balances, prevents fraud, ensures compliance. Critical infrastructure as x402 scales to billions in autonomous AI payments.

### Competitive Landscape
- **Direct x402 competitors:** [TBD - likely none yet on trust layer]
- **Adjacent:** Plaid (banking verification), Stripe (payment processing)
- **Differentiation:** AI-native, x402-specific, trust vs. just processing
