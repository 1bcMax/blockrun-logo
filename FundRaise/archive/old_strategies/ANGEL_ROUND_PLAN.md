## Executive Summary

BlockRun is raising an **angel round of $500K - $1M** at a **$16M post-money valuation** to extend runway through public launch (Jan 2026) and achieve product-market fit metrics required for a Seed/Series A in Q3-Q4 2026.

**Key Thesis:** First-mover advantage in x402 V2 AI gateway space, strategic founder background (Circle Head of Data Science), and clear path to $500M acquisition by 2027.

**Valuation:** $16M post-money

---

## Recommended Deal Terms

### Funding Amount
| Option | Amount | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Target** | **$750K** | 12-15 months runway, hire 1-2 key people |
| Minimum | $500K | 10-12 months runway, lean team |
| Maximum | $1M | 18 months runway, faster scaling |

### Valuation

| Metric | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Post-Money Valuation** | **$16M** |
| Pre-Money Valuation | $15.25M |
| Implied Ownership (at $750K) | 4.7% |

**Valuation Justification:**
1. **Founder Premium:** Former Head of Data Science at Circle (2021-2024) - rare pedigree
2. **Market Timing:** x402 V2 launched Dec 11, 2025 - first mover advantage
3. **Strategic Positioning:** Clear acquisition path to Circle (85% probability per GOAL doc)
4. **Product Readiness:** Private beta live, public launch imminent
5. **Comparable Companies:**
   - Daydreams: Raised at similar stage
   - OpenRouter: Growing rapidly in adjacent space
   - thirdweb: $24M Series A in 2024 for Web3 dev tools

### Instrument Type

**Recommended: SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)**

| SAFE Terms | Recommendation |
|------------|----------------|
| Valuation Cap | $16M |
| Discount | 20% (if uncapped) |
| Pro-rata Rights | Yes, for checks >$50K |
| MFN | Yes |
| Information Rights | Quarterly updates |

**Why SAFE over Priced Round:**
- Faster execution (1-2 weeks vs 4-6 weeks)
- Lower legal costs ($2K vs $15K+)
- Standard for angel/pre-seed in crypto
- Defer valuation negotiation to Seed
