**Response:**
"Fair concern. But x402 hit $600M volume in first 2 months (Nov 2025). Coinbase, Cloudflare, Google Cloud, AWS, Anthropic are already integrated. Even if it's 10% of what we think, that's still a $10B+ market - and we're first-mover on trust infrastructure.

Plus, our AI Gateway is live today - we're not dependent on future adoption. We're building the trust layer as the market grows."

### Objection 2: "Why can't agents just use reputation from Web2?"

**Response:**
"They could, but Web2 reputation doesn't work for blockchain-native payments. You need on-chain verification, real-time fraud detection, and cross-chain trust scoring. Plus, x402 is new - there's no existing reputation system. We're building it from scratch, which is our first-mover advantage."

### Objection 3: "Too early stage - come back for Series A"

**Response:**
"We're live on Base today, not a pitch deck. We have traction, partnerships (AgentKit, GOAT, nofx), and PRs merged into x402 core. If you wait for Series A, you'll pay 3-5x valuation and we'll already have the data moat. The opportunity is now."

### Objection 4: "Why not just integrate with existing services like Stripe?"

**Response:**
"Stripe doesn't do blockchain-native payments, and they don't understand AI agent trust. x402 is USDC micropayments over HTTP - it's a completely new payment primitive. We're building Stripe-level trust infrastructure for this new standard."

### Objection 5: "What if larger players (Coinbase, Circle) build this?"

**Response:**
"They could, but they're focused on payment rails, not trust/reputation. Circle makes money when anyone uses USDC - they're Switzerland. They want someone like us to exist because it makes x402 safer and drives adoption. That's why my former colleagues at Circle are supportive.

Plus, trust is winner-take-most. First to market with data wins."

### Objection 6: "Solana is Q1 - why should I invest now?"

**Response:**
"Because the fundraise closes in Feb, and Solana launch is in March. You're getting in before the ecosystem expansion. Plus, our Base product is already generating data we'll use on Solana - you're investing in the data advantage, not just future launches."

- "We need to discuss internally" without timeline = no urgency
- Asking about far-future projections without discussing process = tire-kicking

- Asking about cap table = serious about terms
- "What's your timeline to close?" = competitive mindset
- Intro to other partners at firm = moving to diligence
- "Can we meet your co-founder?" = validating team
- Specific technical questions = genuine interest
